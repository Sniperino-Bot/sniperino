FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.

Everything you want to know before buying.

Is Sniperino safe? Does anyone have access to my keys? +
No. Sniperino runs completely locally on your machine. Your private keys never leave your computer. There is no cloud server, no external service with wallet access. You have full control.
Which Telegram channels can I monitor? +
Any channel you've joined. Public groups, private VIP channels, personal chats with individual traders — no restrictions. Unlike conventional platform bots, you're not limited to a predefined channel pool. You decide what your alpha source is.
How does payment and access work? +
The entire process is fully automatic. On the purchase page you'll find all payment details. After payment, send your transaction hash and email address via Telegram — you'll automatically receive your download link and license key. No waiting, no manual support needed.
How complicated is the setup? +
You need a Telegram account and a Solana wallet. With the included guide, setup takes about 15 minutes. No coding knowledge required. After that, everything runs automatically.
What if spam or bad calls are posted in my group? +
You configure per group which users the bot reacts to. You can set it to only react to signals from specific users. All other posts are ignored — nobody else can trigger your bot.
Does the bot work after migration to Raydium/PumpSwap? +
Yes. Sniperino detects migrations automatically via Helius and tracks your position on the new pool seamlessly. No manual intervention. Auto-Sell and Stop-Loss work after migration too.
Can I cancel monthly? +
Yes. The license is valid for one month and does not renew automatically. You decide every month. No contract, no subscription trap.
